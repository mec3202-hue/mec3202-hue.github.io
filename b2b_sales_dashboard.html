Strategic Recommendations
Four actions to improve revenue efficiency in 2025
01
Build a 60/90-Day Onboarding Program
The steepest churn occurs between months 3–6. A structured milestone-based onboarding sequence — with check-ins at day 30, 60, and 90 — could improve month-6 retention by an estimated 8–12pp based on industry benchmarks.
02
Shift SMB Acquisition to Self-Serve
SMB LTV:CAC of 2.8× is below the healthy 3× threshold. Introducing a self-serve trial removes high-touch sales costs from the SMB motion, improving unit economics without cutting acquisition volume.
03
Double Down on Mid-Market
Mid-Market has the best LTV:CAC at 6.2× and strong retention. Adding 2 mid-market focused AEs in H1 2025 with a targeted account-based marketing program is the highest-ROI sales investment available.
04
Open an APAC Customer Success Role
APAC grew 62% YoY but currently has no dedicated CS coverage. Without localized support, retention in this region will lag NA/EMEA benchmarks. A regional CSM hire in Q2 2025 would protect this growth.
