Strategic Recommendations
Data-driven actions based on FY 2024 performance
01 · Budget Allocation
Shift 15% of Display budget into Search
Search delivers 4.8x ROAS vs. Display's 2.1x. Reallocating $58K from Display to Search could yield an estimated +$139K in incremental revenue at current efficiency rates.
02 · Seasonal Strategy
Front-load Q4 spend by 3 weeks
Q4 drove 34% of annual revenue but peak spend lagged demand by ~3 weeks. Earlier deployment in mid-October would capture pre-holiday intent at lower CPCs before competition spikes.
03 · Shopping Optimization
Scale Shopping budgets 20–30%
Shopping ROAS jumped from 3.1x to 5.4x after the June feed fix. With product data now clean, increasing Shopping investment is well-justified by current performance efficiency.
04 · Underperformers
Pause or restructure 3 low-ROAS campaigns
Three Display and Video campaigns run below 1.5x ROAS. Pausing these and redirecting spend to top performers would improve blended ROAS from 4.23x to an estimated 4.9x+.
